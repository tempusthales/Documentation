# My Servers

## Dell T430
* **Processors**
  * CPU - Intel® Xeon® processor E5-2600 v4 product family
  * Chipset: Intel C610 series chipset
  * Processor sockets: 2
  * Internal interconnect: Two QPI links at 9.6 GT/s
  * Cache: 2.5MB per core; core options: 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, 14
 
* **Memory**
  * 12 DIMM slots: 4GB/8GB/16GB/32GB DDR4 up to 2400MT/s 
  * 384 GB DDR4 RAM

* **I/O Slots**
  * Support for up to 6 full height slots: 4 x PCIe 3.0, 2 x PCIe 2.0

* **RAID controller**
  * Support for a maximum of 1 internal controller and 1 external controller PERC H730, PERC H730P and PERC H830  
  * **Current RAID Controller**: PERC H830

* **Network controller**
  * 2 x 1GbE LOMs

* **Hard drives**
  * SAS, SATA, nearline SAS, SSD
  * **Current Capacity**: 48 TB

* **Power supply**
  * 495W, 750W, 1100W PSU; 450W cabled non-redundant PSU
  * **Current PSU:** 2x 750W 

* **Availability**
  * Hot-plug drives bays; high-efficiency, hot-plug, redundant power supply units; fan redundancy; extended thermal support; Dell faultresilient memory; Internal dual SD modules
